**SUTRA™** is a family of multilingual AI models developed by **TWO AI**, optimized to support over **50 languages** across a wide variety of advanced tasks. The models in the SUTRA suite are engineered for production-scale performance and include specialized capabilities for:

- 🌍 **Multilingual conversational AI**
- 🧠 **Complex reasoning**
- 📈 **Time-series forecasting**

Each model is tailored to specific real-world use cases, ensuring high accuracy, contextual understanding, and domain-specific excellence.

## ✨ SUTRA-V2

**SUTRA-V2** is designed for multilingual conversation and instruction execution. Trained on a curated blend of proprietary and public datasets, it handles:

- Instruction following
- Customer support bots
- Voice assistants

### Key Features

- Fluency across **Latin**, **Indic**, and **Far Eastern** languages
- Context-aware and natural dialogue
- High accuracy in diverse tasks

### Sample Prompts

> “Summarize this paragraph in Hindi.”

> “What are Newton's laws explained for a 12-year-old in Tamil?”

---

## 🧠 SUTRA-R0

**SUTRA-R0** is the first in a series of reasoning models, enabling structured logic and analytical decision-making.

### Ideal For

- Legal document analysis
- Complex instruction chains
- Business rule applications

### Key Features

- Multi-step reasoning
- Deep contextual understanding
- Strong inference capabilities

### Sample Prompts

> “If Alice is taller than Bob, and Bob is taller than Carol, who’s the tallest?”

> “Explain the tradeoffs between renewable energy sources in simple terms.”

---

## 📈 SUTRA-Q0

**SUTRA-Q0** is a large-scale quantitative model for time-series forecasting and anomaly detection. It supports enterprise-grade decision-making in industries like:

- Finance
- Healthcare
- Logistics

### Key Features

- High-precision predictive analytics
- Dense architecture for fast inference
- Optimized for numerical + temporal data
